CURRICULUM


SSCHS has classes from Nursery to Tenth, It is affiliated to Secondary School Certificate board. English is the medium of Instruction.

Pre-Primary:


Childern have natural tendency and desire to learn. Kindergarten setup childern to naturally learn from exposure to real environment and experiences. Activity-Oriented teaching methods[role-play, dance, songs] helps little minds to better understand the concepts. Play learning and friendly teaching impact little brains positively towards school. Classrooms are made colourful, specially designed to stimulate them and motivate them to explore, play, and learn incidentally.

class first to eighth:

Telugu, Hindi, English, Maths, Science, Social-Studies, Computers

ninth to tenth:


Telugu, Hindi, English, Maths, Phisics, Biology, Social-Studies

ADMISSIONS

School admissions are open in the month of july every year. Sample test is given to students joining school to allot an admission.
Spot admissions are available for pre-primary students in any month of the year.(Terms and conditions apply). For a student previously studied in another school, have to submit transfer certificate, bonofide at the time of admission.
